Questions

The honest answers

Is IntroLinq really free?
Yes, completely. No subscription, no setup fee, no minimum traffic. IntroLinq only takes a share of commission when a booking is completed — as a founder publisher, you keep 90%. If no one books, it costs you nothing, ever.
Is the 90% rate really locked in for life?
Yes. Founder publishers keep 90% of every booking for as long as they use IntroLinq, regardless of how the platform grows or what rate new publishers join at. It's written into your account, not a promotional period.
Will it slow my site down?
No. The IntroLinq script loads asynchronously after your page content. It has zero effect on your page speed score or your readers' experience.
Does it work with any WordPress theme?
Yes. The widget adapts to your site's existing style and works with any standard WordPress theme, including Elementor and Divi.
What if I already have affiliate links on a post?
IntroLinq never interferes with your existing links or monetisation. It only adds expert widgets in content that doesn't already have a relevant link in that context.
How and when do I get paid?
Monthly, via bank transfer or PayPal. You'll receive a clear earnings breakdown with every payment so you know exactly what converted and where.
Can I leave anytime?
Yes. Remove the plugin and you're out. No notice period, no penalty. We'd rather earn your loyalty than lock you in.
