Partnering with IntroLinq

We embed a widget on publisher blogs that recommends your experts to readers, and send you booking-ready traffic. Here's what a partnership needs on your side.

Expert feed

Share your current roster of bookable experts (name, bio, booking link) so we know who to recommend. Most platforms already have this as an API or export.

Attribution cookie

A 30-day cookie on your domain, set from the referral link we send. Credits a booking back to us even if the reader converts days later, or books a different expert.

Booking webhook

One API call to us when a booking completes. If you already track affiliate conversions elsewhere, this is usually a small addition, not new infrastructure.

Payment authorization

A one-time, no-code link to authorize automatic billing - so commission gets collected on a schedule, with no invoices to chase on either side.

Full technical spec: once you're ready to scope the engineering work, /documentation has the exact API contract, request/response examples, and test mode.
